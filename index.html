Digital Culture Project

How analytics changed advertising

Advertising was once built around broad visibility and high-budget reach. Today, it is defined by targeting, measurement, and the ability to respond to audience behavior in real time.

This project follows that shift from traditional brand advertising to paid social and into an AI-driven future. It shows how analytics changed not just how ads are measured, but how campaigns are planned, optimized, and scaled.

Project overview

This project began as a broader look at how data analytics affects digital advertising, but the most compelling part became the progression itself. The story of advertising is really a story of changing access, changing strategy, and changing ways of measuring success.

To make that shift easier to follow, the site is divided into three connected sections. One page explains how advertising evolved over time, one breaks down the metrics that shape decision-making today, and one focuses on how AI is redefining the future of paid media.
