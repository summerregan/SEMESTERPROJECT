How analytics changed advertising

Advertising used to be mostly about getting in front of as many people as possible. Now, it is about getting in front of the right people, learning from behavior, and adjusting fast. This site looks at how we moved from big-budget brand ads to paid social, and now into a future shaped more and more by AI.

Project overview

At first, I planned to talk more broadly about how data analytics affects digital advertising. As I kept researching, though, I realized the story itself was the most interesting part. Advertising has changed so much over time, and I wanted to build something that shows that shift in a way that feels visual, clear, and easy to follow.


This site is broken into different pages so the story feels easier to move through. One page focuses on how advertising evolved over time, one focuses on the key metrics and how brands actually use data, and one focuses on AI and where paid media is heading next.
