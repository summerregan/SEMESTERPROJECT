Source strategy

The project combines platform documentation, industry reporting, and academic research so the argument connects everyday ad tools to broader changes in digital culture.

🤖 A note on AI use in this project

Artificial intelligence, specifically Claude (Anthropic), was used as a tool throughout this project. AI assisted with layout design, content refinement, HTML/CSS structure, and helping me clarify and sharpen my analysis. All research, sources, arguments, and perspective are my own. AI was a production and editing tool, not an author or thinker.

Using AI in this project actually became part of its subject matter. Learning how to prompt, direct, and critically evaluate AI generated output gave me firsthand experience with the kind of human AI collaboration that is reshaping marketing workflows. That experience directly informed the AI + Future section of this site. AI opened up the opportunity to build something at a level of polish I wouldn't have reached on my own in the given time frame, which is itself an example of what this project is about.
